## How to Work

1. **Describe your task** — The Conductor (CEO) will triage and create a contract
2. **Review the contract** — Approve, modify, or reject before execution begins
3. **Watch the team work** — Workers execute in parallel via DAG resolution
4. **Get results** — Verified output with full audit trail
